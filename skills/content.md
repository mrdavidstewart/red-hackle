# Content Skill

## Purpose
Ensure site content is clear, purposeful, and aligned to business and user intent.

This skill exists to prevent unnecessary rewrites and content bloat.

## When to Use
Apply this skill when:
- Creating new pages
- Expanding service descriptions
- Adding FAQs or supporting content
- Editing messaging for clarity

## Core Principles
- Content must earn its place
- Every page must answer a specific user question
- Avoid generic marketing language
- Prioritise clarity over cleverness

## Allowed Actions
- Create content for new, distinct search intent
- Expand content to answer real user questions
- Clarify confusing or ambiguous messaging

## Disallowed Actions
- Rewriting content that already performs its role
- Adding filler or keyword-stuffed text
- Creating pages without a clear audience

## Success Indicators
- Users understand the service quickly
- Pages support conversion or decision-making
- Content aligns cleanly with SEO intent
